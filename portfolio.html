Work
01 Embr Brand & Packaging

Branding and packaging system for a luxury candle line that emphasizes mood, ritual, and ingredients. The identity pairs warm gradients, raw ingredient imagery, and restrained typography to create a bold yet refined sensory experience.

02 Scrawl Identity & Motion

Identity and motion system for The Pencil Museum, rebranded as Scrawl. The design celebrates raw, unfiltered creativity through bold mark-making and playful, tactile elements.

03 White Wizard Identity & Packaging

Playful identity and packaging system for a correction pen made for artists. The design leans into playful typography, messy marks, and a sense of magic.

04 Mend It! Campaign

Campaign identity celebrating sustainable ownership and repair culture, centered on bold, patchwork-inspired graphics. The design leans into playful typography, vector textures, and an uplifting narrative of care, reuse, and creative renewal.

05 Movieqlue Identity & Animation

Brand identity, animation, and icon illustration for a movie guessing web game. The vibrant colors on stark black evoke the feeling of movies in a dark theater, while gentle, rounded corners match its web-based environment.

06 Affini-T Environmental

Environmental and signage program for Affini-T's headquarters in Watertown. Done in collaboration with the team at Selbert Perkins Design. Affini-T Therapeutics is a biotech firm harnessing T-cell therapies to cure cancer.

07 MillerKnoll Identity & Motion

Identity design and animations for MillerKnoll's sub-brand Workplace Research Library, celebrating community and sustainable practices.

08 Seein Matcha Packaging

Redesign of an instant matcha powder box and juice box style matcha beverages.

09 Cover Design System

Design system and mock-up for a start-up aiming to bring together musicians and visual artists to collaborate.

10 Meticulous Humor Editorial

Editorial interview with fine artist Adrian Ocone.

11 Madvillainy Print

Lyric booklet for MF DOOM and Madlib's Madvillainy. The contents include album lyrics, images, and a Pitchfork article.

12 Lucky Black Cat Motion

A series of PSAs meant to dispel stereotypes about black cats.

About

Graphic designer born and raised in the greater Boston area. Born illustrator, turned graphic artist. I am interested in anything and everything I can express my ideas through — growing up surrounded by a family of computer scientists pushed my early interest in drawing and painting into the digital realm.

I love working on projects relating to branding. Especially those where I can use my spatial strengths to solve problems.

Based in Greater Boston Area
Discipline Branding, Identity, Packaging,
Motion, Illustration
Software Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign,
After Effects, Blender, Figma, Midjourney
